Start writing again

January, 2023
Bellevue, WA

A very short section. Just some quick thoughts on adding more to my website. It's been definitely a long time since I last updated this site. There are multitudes of reasons and excuses but mostly boils down to I didn't have much I wanted to write down.

But I think that even if there aren't any audience besides myself, it would be nice just to have a record of the thoughts and ideas I have now and look back at a later time. Sure the content would be pretty random and maybe not the best researched. But it still is a moment in time in my life that I can look back many years later.

Man has two great gifts: ‘words’ to give sound to thoughts, and ‘writing’ to give thoughts meaning forever.

― Gianfranco Iovino

That's it, that the extent of this post.

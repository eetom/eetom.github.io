The distance between imagination and reality

May 12th, 2026
Bellevue, WA

Ever watch some youtube, TED talk, or see some cool projects online and thought, "wow that is so cool. I want to try to do that". From my own experience, the people who give me that feeling the most are who I call "Builders". These are folks that have a combination of craft and execution of building something. It doesn't always have to be the most complex machinery or advance code. Sometimes, I'm just in awe of simple designs, rought concepts, and prototypes that don't work. It's not about the finished prodcut, it's about building the first version, and then the next and then the next ..

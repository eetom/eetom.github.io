A design system designed to empower designers

The style guide project is a multi faceted project with collaboration and initiatives from every designer on the team. The goal is for us to improve quality and consistency of design work by setting standards and guidelines based on values that would best represent our team and work. This includes all aspects of the design process from research to implementation and even help the front end UI team build and reuse design components.

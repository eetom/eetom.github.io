TELEBORDER WEB APP DESIGN

Teleborder (YC S13) is an online platform that allows companies and expats work through the work visa / immigration process. We work closely with service providers on the platform to increase efficiency and accuracy of information for a better experience for the customers.

The app is designed to be very easy to use, but is very complicated and multfaceted as a whole. For each type of user, there is their own specific interface that allows them to manage their part of the process. Employees have a very simplistic dashboard that focuses on getting their documents online, company dashboard is about managing their employees and progress of visa applications.

TELEBORDER REBRAND & LOGO DESIGN

While I was working on app and websites, I was also planning out the rebranding for Teleborder. The logo and branding had languished and need a quick update to tie all the new ideas and products of the comapny together. The branding was stepped out in stages, first going through the CSS front end to make things set to a consistent color and font base, then I set about creating a new logo that represented the connectivity and modularity of our system.

ANDROID APP

Zen Wallpaper HD



APP UI DESIGN

Zen Wallpaper HD is a personal project that I collaborated with my friend to build in a month as a challenge. We designed the app in Illustrator and then built the app on Cordova wrapped Android.

This app was a chance for me to try out more interesting interface design, such as adding gestures so that the app can be used one handed and a menu that was sorted by feelings evoked from the colours.

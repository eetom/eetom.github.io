Runscope Marketing Design

Runscope's core mission is to inform their clients in realtime the uptime status of complex APIs that are being monitored.

This is a relatively technical task, and communicating it visually has consistently been a challenge. The most effective method of communicating technical ideas has been through logical and aesthetic pleasing visualizations.

That is the angle I took with the Runescope marketing design, using a combination of technical visualizations consistent with their brand image and distilling complex interactions into bite sized picto-graphs.
